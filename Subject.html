Subject

Introduction to Computing

This foundational subject provides an overview of the fundamental concepts in computing, preparing students for their journey in the field of Information Technology. Offered at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Pasig, the course introduces the basics of computer systems, programming, data management, and the role of technology in solving real-world problems.

The history and evolution of computers.

Basic hardware and software components.

The subject aims to build a solid understanding of how computers work and their applications in various industries, equipping students with the skills to adapt to the fast-changing tech landscape.
