Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) The BSIT program at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Pasig is designed to equip students with the technical skills and knowledge needed to excel in the field of Information Technology. This course focuses on the development, implementation, and management of software applications, networks, and IT systems, preparing graduates for the demands of the digital era.

Students will gain hands-on experience in programming, database management, web and mobile application development, and cybersecurity. The program also emphasizes problem-solving, critical thinking, and innovation, ensuring that graduates are ready to meet the challenges of the IT industry.
